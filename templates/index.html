Our Story

Founded in 2015, Coffee Bliss began as a small cart in downtown. Our passion for perfect coffee led us to open this cozy shop where every cup is brewed with love and precision.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms and roast them in-house to bring you the freshest flavors possible. Each batch is carefully selected and roasted to perfection.
